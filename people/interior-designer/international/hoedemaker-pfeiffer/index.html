Hoedemaker Pfeiffer

Multidisciplinary architecture and interiors studio creating modern yet timeless residential experiences, including renovations, new construction, and interiors.

Instagram Website

Location: Seattle

Hoedemaker Pfeiffer is a strong reference for architecture-led interiors: modern but timeless homes where renovation, new construction, and interior design are treated together. Their featured work includes projects like The Bluff, The Snug, Arrow Bay, and South Beach, which makes this useful for studying complete houses rather than isolated decorated rooms. Save for calm Pacific Northwest sensibility, architectural restraint, layered materials, and homes where the exterior, plan, and interior mood feel integrated.
