HÁM Interiors

British family-run architecture, build, joinery, interiors, and decoration studio with layered, lived-in rooms, confident color, antiques, art, and natural materials.

Instagram Website

Location: London

HÁM is useful as a reference for rooms that feel collected rather than newly decorated: old and new together, confident color, pattern, antiques, art, and natural materials. Their work spans architecture, build, joinery, interiors, decoration, and commercial creative direction, so this is a good source for studying how the shell of a room, custom details, and styling all work together. Save especially for country-house warmth, townhouses, pubs, bespoke joinery, and that “lived-in, loved, beautifully made” feeling. They also have Studio HÁM, a home and lifestyle store, so this may later deserve a duplicate store entry for furniture, lighting, accessories, art, antiques, and one-off finds.
