LA Closet Design

Luxury closet design studio by Lisa Adams, focused on full-service custom closet design, organization, storage accessories, and polished wardrobe spaces.

Instagram Website

Location: LA

LA Closet Design is a focused reference for the storage side of interiors. Lisa Adams’ studio has offered full-service luxury closet design since 2007, with locations/pages for Los Angeles, NYC, Nashville, Chicago, Miami, Dallas, and Aspen. Save for closet systems, dressing rooms, storage accessories, organization, wardrobe preservation, and sleek built-in ideas that calm visual clutter. This could also become a future Stores / Objects / Home reference because the shop offers storage accessories.
