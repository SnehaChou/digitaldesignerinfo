Kim Lapin Interiors

Los Angeles interior designer and decorator known for eclectic, joyful spaces layered with color, texture, vintage pieces, and showroom finds.

Instagram Website

Location: LA

Kim Lapin Interiors is a good reference for Southern California ease with real personality. Her site describes her as a Los Angeles interior designer and decorator who believes homes should reflect who people are, what they love, and how they want to live. Save for eclectic and joyful rooms, high/low mixing, vintage with showroom pieces, architectural interest, layered colors and textures, and projects ranging from residences to commercial spaces.
