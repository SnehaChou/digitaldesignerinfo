Heritage House by Kelsey Leigh Design Co., combining interior-design project collections with furniture, lighting, rugs, decor, and made-to-order upholstery.

This entry is the Heritage House side of the KLD world: an online shop and project archive for furniture, lighting, rugs, art, decor, made-to-order pieces, and room-by-room shopping. The site is useful for seeing how a designer’s finished projects become shoppable collections like Rustic Refined, Heritage House Cottage, Black Oak, and Cowboy Cool.
