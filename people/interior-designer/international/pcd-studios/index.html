PCD Studios

New York and West Palm Beach residential interior design and cabinetry studio blending bespoke cabinetry, antiques, modern design, tradition, and minimalism.

Instagram Website

Location: NYC

PCD Studios is a strong reference for cabinetry-led luxury interiors. Their site describes a mix of bespoke cabinetry, hand-picked antiques, modern design, craftsmanship, and intentional detail, with offices in New York and West Palm Beach. Save for rooms where cabinetry is the backbone, historical influence meets contemporary function, and traditional details are pared back into a modern-minimal result.
