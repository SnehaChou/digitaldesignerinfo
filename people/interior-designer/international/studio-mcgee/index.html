Studio McGee

Modern-traditional design house by Shea McGee, combining portfolio work, lifestyle content, books, and McGee & Co. home furnishings.

Instagram Website

Location: Utah

Studio McGee is a major reference for the “aspirational but attainable” American home look: warm, layered, collected, comfortable, and modern-traditional. The site frames the current aesthetic as New Heritage, rooted in classic principles and traditional craftsmanship through a modern lens. Save for full portfolio browsing, Oxford Road-type project series, paint stories, family-friendly entertaining spaces, and the link between Studio McGee design work and McGee & Co. furniture and decor.
