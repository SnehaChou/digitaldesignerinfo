Véronique Savigné

Cape Town style reference centered on soulful, richly hued, patinaed interiors layered with antiques, art, curiosities, textiles, and travel finds.

Instagram Website

Location: Cape Town

Véronique Savigné is saved here through an Aspire feature on her Cape Town home. The article is rich with ideas: soul over polish, antiques and curiosities, U-shaped layout around a courtyard, transitional indoor-outdoor spaces, hand-knotted rugs, vintage textiles, reed ceilings, poplar beams, sun-baked clay color, African contemporary art, and rooms designed for gathering. Save for warmth, patina, texture, courtyard living, worldly collections, and homes that feel full of life.
