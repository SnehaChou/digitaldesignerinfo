The site organizes furniture, lighting, rugs, art, decor, room shopping, KLD project collections, made-to-order upholstery, and Kelsey McGregor’s vintage-inspired lighting collaboration with Alora Mood. Save for layered lived-in rooms, rustic-refined styling, warm natural light, rich color, modern European touches, and practical “shop the room” sourcing.
