NYDC is more of a design-resource hub than a designer. The New York Design Center at 200 Lex houses nearly 100 showrooms representing more than 500 lines across traditional, contemporary, residential, and contract furniture, plus fabric, floor covering, lighting, wallcovering, kitchen and bath, and decorative accessory resources. Save for showroom discovery, product sourcing, textiles, antiques, trade resources, events, and “where did this designer source that?” research.
